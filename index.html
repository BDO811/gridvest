01 · How it works

Hold cash, buy dips

Most of your portfolio stays in cash reserve. When prices fall, the engine deploys small, fixed-size blocks at pre-planned rungs below the market. Each block is a limit order. No chasing, no guessing.

Sell the rebound

Every filled block carries a sell order at a fixed profit target from the moment it is bought. When the market recovers, blocks close one by one and the cash returns to reserve, ready for the next swing.

Repeat, on your schedule

Each morning the app reads your broker activity, tells you which orders to cancel and which to place, and tracks every block from open to close. The whole routine fits in a coffee break.

Built to be yours

GridVest is fully self-directed and fully configurable. You choose the funds, the target allocations, the cash reserve, and the risk level. The engine handles the arithmetic and the discipline.

Your money never leaves your own brokerage accounts. GridVest never holds funds, never places trades for you, and never sees your broker password.

03 · Risk, stated plainly

This strategy trades leveraged index funds. They are volatile by design and can fall much further and faster than the market itself. Drawdowns of half or more of a position's value have happened and will happen again. A ladder of small purchases and a large cash reserve reduce exposure; they do not remove it. Never invest money you cannot leave invested through a long recovery.

Spotlight on key topics

Is something wrong if I have no new buy orders today?

No. New rungs only appear when price sits inside the ladder window and your deployment is under its cap. Quiet days are normal and by design.

What do I do if my sell order expires?

Tomorrow's Daily Processing will re-issue the sell at the same target above the block's basis. Nothing is lost by an expiry.

What happens if I miss a day?

The engine reconciles from your next broker import. Open blocks keep their targets; you simply may have missed placing a rung.

How do I move money in or out?

Deposit or withdraw at your broker as usual, then import the CSV. Contributions and withdrawals are tracked as separate line items so returns stay accurate.
